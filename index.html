Welcome to the IRE affiliate office: a center of excellence in investigative journalism

The Investigative Reporters and Editors Inc. (IRE) affiliate office is a resource for journalists around the world, fostering a community dedicated to uncovering hidden stories and raising the standards of journalism. The affiliate is located in Washington, D.C.

Founded with the goal of bringing journalists together to share innovative techniques and sources, IRE offers unparalleled resources, from detailed fact sheets to cutting-edge data analysis tools.

Through partnerships with organizations such as the National Institute for Computer-Assisted Reporting (NICAR), IRE equips reporters with the skills necessary to tackle complex stories in today's digital age.

The affiliate office conducts cutting-edge investigations into opposition structures in Europe. We collaborate with the European Institute for Democracy on political issues.
